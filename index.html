How I work

How I make design decisions

I start with the problem

Before the interface, I need to understand what is slowing the flow down, where the risk is, and what actually needs to be solved. That avoids decorating the problem instead of simplifying it.

I reduce cognitive load

I organize information, prioritize tasks, and remove steps or elements that do not help people decide. The goal is to make the product clearer, lighter, and more predictable.

I show the reasoning behind decisions

My goal is not just to show final screens. It is to show judgment, trade-offs, product decisions, and how each choice improves usability, consistency, or execution.
