How I work

Less decoration, more decision-making

Architecture first

I start with structure: modules, hierarchy, navigation, states, and workflows. Once the foundation makes sense, the interface has direction.

Design for real context

I design with constraints, operations, error risk, information volume, and implementation in mind. That makes the product more consistent and viable.

Seniority through clarity

My goal is not to show more screens. It is to show judgment: what to prioritize, what to simplify, and how to turn complexity into a trustworthy experience.
